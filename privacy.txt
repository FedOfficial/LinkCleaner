Privacy Policy for CNFans Ref Injector Extension

This browser extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All operations are performed locally within the user's browser. The extension:
- Removes popup warnings and overlays on supported shopping agent websites.
- Adds or updates referral parameters in CNFans product links.

No user activity, browsing history, or personal information is collected or transmitted.

If you have any concerns or questions about this policy, please reach out to the developer.

Effective date: 2025-05-28
